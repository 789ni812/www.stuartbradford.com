We live in crazy time where technology is impacting every aspect of our lives. The rate of change is accelerating and it is becoming increasingly difficult to keep up with the latest developments. The purpose of this table is to provide a simple overview of the most important technologies that are likely to have a significant impact on our lives in the next 10-20 years.

Inspired by the Periodic Table of Elements by Mendeleev, each of the 100 technologies was given a 2-letter abbreviation, followed by a brief description. The x-axis reports time and the y-axis indicates the potential disruption of the technology if it was adopted on a large scale.
